Apple Pie

Pies are great, I think we can all agree on that. Weather it be a fruit pie, or a cream pie, there
is always room for a pie at the dinner table. For every party my family through, we always
brought a pie. hell, I still get one for my birthday every year!

I recommend everyone learn to bake at least one type of pie. They are easy to bake, and then you will
finally be the life of the party like you always hoped for!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 lbs. baking apples sliced into 1/2-inch wedges
  • 2 tablespoons sugar, plust more for sprinkling
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour, plus more for dusting
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • Pie Dough
  • 1 large egg white, beaten

Steps

  1. IN A large bowl, toss the apples with the sugar, flour,
    and spices. Set aside
  2. TRANSFER THE Dough to a foured work surface. dust the
    dough with four and pound it lightly with a rolling pin. Divide
    it into two equal pieces and gently roll one out to a 13-inch
    round. Roll the dough round onto your rolling pin and unroll
    it over a 9-inch pie plate. Without stretching the dough, ease it
    into the pie plate, leaving a 1-inch overhang all around. Wrap
    the pie plate with plastic and refrigerate for 10-15 minutes. Roll
    the second piece of dough out to a 10-inch round and cut it into
    1-inch-wide strips for the lattice top.
  3. PLACE AN aluminum baking sheet in the oven and preheat
    the oven to 500F. Transfer the apples to the pie plate and press
    down gently. Place 5 of the lattice strips across the top of your
    pie, spacing them evenly, lifting 2 strips at a time and interwe-
    aving the strips in a opposite direction. Trim off the excess do-
    ugh and tuck it underneath the edge. Crimp the edge every inch
    or so using your fingers. Brush the edge and lattice with beaten
    egg white and sprinkle sugar over the top. Transfer the pie plate
    to the preheated baking sheet, reduce the oven temperature to
    400F and bake until the crust is deep brown and crisp. 30-45 min.
    Remove from oven and let cool on a wire rack for at least 4 hours.
  4. SLICE INTO wedges and serve.
